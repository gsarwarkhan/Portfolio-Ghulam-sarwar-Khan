About My Journey

From Business Administration to AI Innovation

My professional journey began in the dynamic world of trade and commerce, where I served as a Deputy Secretary at the Karachi Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI). I believe in the power of technology to transform business processes and drive organizational growth. This philosophy guides my approach to both business and technology, ensuring that every solution I create serves not just functional needs, but also elevates organizational efficiency and competitive advantage.
